ABOUT THE EVENT

What is DawsHacks?

DawsHacks is Dawson College's premier hackathon — a 10-hour event where students come together to turn bold ideas into real-world tech solutions. Whether you're a seasoned coder or a curious beginner, join us to collaborate, build, and innovate alongside fellow creators.

Code & Create

Build projects using cutting-edge technologies and tools.

Team Up

Collaborate with talented students from top colleges and universities.

Learn & Grow

Practice your coding skills.

Network

Connect with other student and mentors.

Free Food

Enjoy meals and snacks throughout the event.

Win Prizes

Showcase your project to judges and win valuable prizes
